# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Focus on technical clarity, not personal attacks.
3. Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
4. Keep discussions inclusive and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior
1. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or threats.
2. Personal attacks, trolling, or doxxing.
3. Repeated disruptive behavior after feedback.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, lock discussions, or restrict participation when standards are violated.

## Scope
This applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and project communication channels.
