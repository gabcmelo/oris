# Product Vision

SafeGuild (project name Voz do Povo) is an open-source, self-hosted communication platform focused on safety, transparency, and usability.

Primary users:
1. Communities of gamers/streamers.
2. Dev/geek communities.
3. Teams needing private self-hosted communication.

Vision principles:
1. Safe by default.
2. Easy to self-host.
3. Open and auditable architecture.
4. Incremental delivery with practical MVP milestones.
